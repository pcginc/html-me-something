Road trip & pics

Last August I went on a 14 day road trip out West. These are a few of the things I saw and did:

Sturgis Rally:

Our first unplanned stop was at the Sturgis Rally. There were bikes as far as the eye could see, and towards the beginning and the end all the bikes lined up down main street for a photo.

Ranch:

We arrived at our ranch house which had a driveway that was more than a few miles long.

Kayaking:

Our first day at the ranch we drove to Yellowstone and decided to rent kayaks for the day.

Grand Canyon:

We later traveled down to see the grand canyon.
